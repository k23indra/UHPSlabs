In UHPS

Comprehensive Device Characterization

Analyzing static and dynamic behaviors of power devices. We utilize diverse measurement platforms to evaluate switching characteristics and dynamic parameters for power devices.

Advanced Power Device Design

Designing next-generation power semiconductors across various materials and structures. We focus on optimizing device physics and comprehensive structural design using advanced TCAD modeling to overcome material limits.

Module, System & Reliability

Expanding research from discrete devices to modules and system-level applications. We investigate device characteristics under specific conditions to ensure reliability and performance in harsh environments.
