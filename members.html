Thank you for visiting the website of the Power Semiconductor Research Lab at the Korea Institute of Energy Technology (KENTECH). We are currently living in the era of an "Energy Revolution." The proliferation of electric vehicles (EVs), smart grids, and renewable energy systems demands more efficient and intelligent power control technologies. To meet these global demands, our lab aims to accelerate the realization of a Carbon-free future through advanced Power Semiconductor technologies that manage energy with maximum efficiency behind the scenes. Beyond merely creating high-performance semiconductors, we conduct our research with the pride that our technologies contribute to protecting the global environment and bridging the gap in energy inequality. Upholding KENTECH’s core values of "Excellence" and "Coexistence," we strive to become a "First Mover" in the global energy semiconductor sector through creative research that breaks conventional boundaries. We look forward to your continued interest and support.
